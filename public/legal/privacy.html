Privacy Policy

Saver’s Med Pte Ltd — Last updated: August 14, 2025

Saver’s Med Pte Ltd (“Saver’s Med”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to the protection of personal data in accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (“PDPA”). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard personal data through our websites (including colonaiq-asia.com and saversmed.com), forms and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Personal Data We Collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Identifiers & contact details (e.g., name, email, phone, address). We do not request NRIC unless required by law.
  • Health-related information you choose to provide in enquiries (kept confidential and used only to address your request).
  • Technical & usage data (e.g., IP address, device/browser type, pages viewed) collected via cookies/analytics.
  • Communications with us (emails, web forms, call notes).

2. Purpose of Use

  • Provide, operate and improve the Services.
  • Respond to enquiries (patients, clinics/labs, investors) and provide customer support.
  • Arrange partner onboarding and manage listings of certified test providers.
  • Send important notices about the Services or policy changes.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud/abuse, and comply with laws and regulations.
  • Analytics to understand site usage and improve content (aggregated and de‑identified where possible).

3. Consent, Access & Correction

By using our Services or submitting personal data, you consent to our collection, use and disclosure as described here. You may withdraw consent, or request access to and correction of your personal data, by emailing our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at admin@saversmed.com. We will respond within a reasonable time (generally within 30 days).

4. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies (e.g., for basic traffic analytics and to remember preferences). You can control cookies via your browser settings. We do not use cookies to serve personalised advertising on our medical pages.

5. Disclosure & International Transfers

We may disclose personal data to service providers (e.g., website hosting, customer support tools) solely to perform services for us. Where personal data is transferred outside Singapore, we ensure that the recipient provides a standard of protection comparable to the PDPA, including via contractual safeguards.

6. Protection & Retention

We implement reasonable administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, disposal or similar risks. We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or to meet legal/operational requirements, after which it is anonymised or securely deleted.

7. Do Not Call (DNC) Preferences

If your Singapore telephone number is in the National DNC Registry, we will not send marketing messages or make telemarketing calls to that number unless you have given clear consent. Transactional and service messages may still be sent where permitted by law.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are intended for adults. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a minor has provided personal data to us without consent, please contact the DPO so we can take appropriate action.

9. Third‑Party Links

The Services may contain links to external sites not operated by us. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies.

10. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
